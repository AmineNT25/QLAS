# QLAS — Business Discovery

**Search Google Maps. Get auto-scored outreach opportunities. No account needed.**

QLAS started as an internal prospecting tool and has been reworked into a free, public, no-login demo: type a business category and a city, and it scrapes Google Maps, scores each result for outreach potential, and shows you the list — nothing to sign up for.

## What it does

- **Business Discovery** — enter a category ("restaurants", "gyms", "clinics") and a city, and QLAS scrapes Google Maps for matching businesses.
- **Opportunity Scoring** — each result is automatically scored on outreach potential (review count, rating, online presence signals, etc.).
- **Recent Searches** — past searches and their results are saved and browsable, no login required.
